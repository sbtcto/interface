Benefits for Merchants

The network fee for a typical SBitcoin transaction is less than one penny. If you want to convert your SBTC into fiat currency, such as US dollars, you can do that through merchant processors for a cost that is still much lower than credit card processing.

Unlike credit cards, there are never any automatic voids, refunds, chargebacks, or other unexpected fees. Fraud protection is built into the system with no cost to the merchant.
